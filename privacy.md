# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 27 May 2026**

This policy explains what data Settle: Meditation Journal collects, why, where it's stored, and what control you have over it.

The App is operated by Simon Fleet. Contact: [simongabrielfleet@gmail.com](mailto:simongabrielfleet@gmail.com).

## What we collect

When you create an account and use the App, we store:

- **Account information** — your email address and an encrypted password hash.
- **Meditation session records** — start time, duration, the technique or timer settings you used, and any notes, ratings, or quality metrics you enter.
- **Influence logs** — anything you log as having influenced a session (for example caffeine, sleep, mood) and the values you record.
- **Custom configuration** — the quality metrics, reminders, and preferences you set up inside the App.
- **Periodic reviews** — any written reflections you save.

We do not collect your name, location, contacts, photos, microphone audio, advertising identifiers, or any analytics about how you tap around the App.

## How we use it

We use the data above only to:

- Authenticate you and keep you signed in across devices
- Display your meditation history, trends, and saved reviews back to you
- Deliver the reminder notifications you've configured

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to train any model.

## Where it's stored

Your account and meditation data are stored in a Supabase Postgres database. Supabase is our backend provider and acts as a data processor on our behalf. You can read their security and compliance documentation at [supabase.com/security](https://supabase.com/security).

Your data is also cached locally on your device so the App works offline. Local data is removed when you delete the App.

## Your rights

- **Access** — every piece of data we hold about you is shown to you inside the App.
- **Deletion** — open **Settings → Account → Delete account** inside the App. This permanently removes your account, every meditation record, and all associated data. The action is immediate and cannot be undone.
- **Export** — email [simongabrielfleet@gmail.com](mailto:simongabrielfleet@gmail.com) and we'll send you a copy of your data within 30 days.
- **Correction** — most fields can be edited directly in the App. For anything else, email us.

## Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data is removed immediately from our database. Backups roll off within 30 days.

## Children

The App is not intended for anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, email us and we will delete it.

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify you by email before they take effect.
